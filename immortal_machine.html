IMMORTAL MACHINE

Home Research Art C.V.

In 2019 I was commissioned by General Electric to produce 5 looping animations. The purpose of these animations is to visualise the systems of autonomous machines that might one day constitute the Internet of Things. In these systems, each machine can communicate and harvest data from each other and use that data to continuously update themselves and the objects they manufacture.
