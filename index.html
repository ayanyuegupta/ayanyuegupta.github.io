Ayan-Yue Gupta

I am a PhD sociology student at the University of Bristol. My thesis is an investigation into using large language models as a tool for addressing the concerns of Foucauldian discourse analysis. My research interests cover computational social science, governmentality, natural language processing, pragmatics, philosophy of language and discourse analysis.

Before starting my PhD I was a practising artist in London. To see my art works visit my instagram and youtube pages.
